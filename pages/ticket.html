TICKETING

Welcome to the Ticketing page! Here, you'll find all the information you need to secure your spot at our upcoming pool party. We offer a variety of ticket options to suit different preferences and budgets, ensuring everyone can join in the fun. Below are the details about ticket prices, types, purchasing options, and special offers.

Ticket Prices and Types

We offer several types of tickets to cater to different needs:

  1. Early Bird Tickets:
    • Price: Rs. 1600
    • Availability: Limited time offer until a specified date.
    • Benefits: Discounted rate for early purchasers.
  2. General Admission Tickets:
    • Price: Rs. 2500
    • Availability: Available until the day of the event or until sold out.
    • Benefits: Access to all general areas and activities of the pool party.
  3. VIP Tickets:
    • Price: Rs. 4000
    • Availability: Limited quantity.
    • Benefits: Includes access to exclusive VIP areas, complimentary drinks, and special seating.
  4. Group Tickets:
    • Price: Rs. 2000 per person (minimum purchase of 5 tickets).
    • Availability: Available until the day of the event or until sold out.
    • Benefits: Discounted rate for groups, making it perfect for friends and family attending together.

Online Ticket Purchasing Option

Purchasing tickets online is quick and easy:

  • Step 1: Visit our Online Ticket Portal (link to be provided).
  • Step 2: Select the type and quantity of tickets you wish to purchase.
  • Step 3: Fill in the required information and proceed to payment.
  • Step 4: Receive your e-tickets via email, which you can print or save on your mobile device.

Discounts and Special Offers

We value our attendees and offer several discounts and special promotions:

  • Early Bird Discount: Save Rs. 800 on General Admission tickets by purchasing before the early bird deadline.
  • Group Discounts: Enjoy a reduced rate of Rs. 2000 per ticket when purchasing five or more tickets together.
  • Student Discount: Students with a valid ID can purchase General Admission tickets for Rs. 2000. (ID to be presented at the entrance).
  • Social Media Contest: Participate in our social media contest for a chance to win free VIP tickets. Follow us on social media for more details.

We recommend purchasing tickets early to take advantage of these offers and secure your place at the event. Should you have any questions or need assistance with your purchase, please contact us through the provided contact form or reach out via our social media channels.
